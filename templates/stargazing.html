Stargazing in the High Desert

The high desert offers unparalleled stargazing opportunities due to its dark skies and clear atmosphere. Here's what you need to know to make the most of your stargazing experience:

What to Bring

  • Telescope or binoculars (optional)
  • Red light flashlight (to preserve night vision)
  • Blanket or chair
  • Warm clothes (even in the desert, nights can be cold)
  • Star chart or app
