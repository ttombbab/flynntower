Welcom to The Kiva

In earthen womb, a shadowed round,
A kiva's heart, where peace is found.
Ladders rise, to sunlit air,
But spirits dwell, in depths of prayer.

Fire's glow, on painted wall,
Ancestral whispers, heed the call.
From dusty floor, to smoke-filled dome,
A sacred space, a spirit home.
